Things to check when, your system seems to be acting strange. (In order ... somewhat... but not necessarily)

  1. Check for Viruses/Trojans/Worms
    • The (as mentioned many places on my site here) are Kaspersky Antivirus or Bit Defender
    • The rest (not necessarily all of them though, tend to be "patchy" in how good their detection actually is
    • Mcafee and Norton in my option are the two worst
    • Some people swear by their anti-trojan scanners too (The Cleaner, for instance), but any virus scanner worth anything if you ask me, will detect most trojans or worms
